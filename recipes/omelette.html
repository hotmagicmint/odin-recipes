Omelette

Description

An omelette is a dish made from beaten eggs that are cooked in a pan, often folded or rolled, and filled with various ingredients like cheese, vegetables, or meat.

It's a versatile dish, commonly enjoyed for breakfast but suitable for any meal. The texture can range from fluffy and soft to firm, depending on the cooking style and ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper
  2. Melt butter over medium heat
  3. Add eggs
  4. Stir briefly, then lift up edges and tilt pan to let the egg run under
  5. Sprinkle with cheese
  6. Once the top is set, fold and serve
