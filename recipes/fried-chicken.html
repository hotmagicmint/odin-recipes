Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is a dish made by coating chicken pieces in a seasoned batter or breading and then deep-frying them until golden brown and crispy.

The resulting chicken is characterized by a crispy exterior and a juicy, tender interior. Flavor profiles vary depending on the seasonings used, but it's generally savory and slightly salty.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Flour
  • Seasonings (salt,pepper,paprika)
  • Buttermilk
  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in buttermilk
  2. Mix the seasoned flour
  3. Add the chicken pieces, one at a time, and turn to coat them in seasoned flour
  4. Let it rest
  5. Deep-fry the chicken
