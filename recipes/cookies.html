Cookies

Description

A cookie, in the context of food, is a small, flat, and sweet baked good, often round in shape.

Cookies are commonly enjoyed as a snack or dessert.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips
  • Baking soda

Steps

  1. Beat sugar, butter, egg and vanilla in one bowl
  2. Mix flour and baking soda in another bowl
  3. Gradually blend the flour mixture into the butter mixture
  4. Add chocolate chips
  5. Roll the dough into balls
  6. Bake until the edges are golden
